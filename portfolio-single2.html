Hungry Groundhog

DESCRIPTION

This 3D game is developed using the Unity game engine, a popular choice for game development. The primary scripting language used for building the game mechanics and interactions is C#. In this immersive gaming experience, players find themselves in a captivating world where they face off against formidable foes known as goblins.

One of the standout features of this game is the intelligent enemy AI (Artificial Intelligence). The goblin enemies are programmed to exhibit behaviors that make them seem eerily real. They possess the ability to detect the player within a certain radius. When a player enters this radius, the goblins spring into action, pursuing the player relentlessly.

The ultimate goal of the player is to navigate through the game's intricately designed landscapes, braving dangers and outsmarting goblins. The key to success lies in collecting enough resources and safely reaching their home base, the 'house.' Victory is achieved when players successfully deliver the collected items to their house. In summary, this Unity-based 3D game offers an exciting blend of immersive gameplay, challenging AI-driven adversaries, and resource management. It promises an engaging and rewarding experience where strategic thinking, quick reflexes, and wit are the keys to triumph."
